Why I Built This

6 years of watching people
struggle in the gym.

I'm Harshit — a software engineer, father, and gym-goer based in New Jersey. I've spent 6+ years working out at commercial gyms, at my EMS station, and in hotel rooms when traveling for work.

In that time I've watched hundreds of people walk into gyms with real motivation — and walk out months later, frustrated and unchanged. Not because they lacked commitment. Because nobody gave them the right guidance at the right time.

I built Exercitron to be the coach I wish I'd had when I started — and the one I still want now.

Smart Coaching

Know exactly when to push.
And when to recover.

Most people plateau because nobody tells them when to increase weight — or when they're lifting too heavy to make progress. Exercitron solves both.

Real coaching messages from Exercitron
🏆
Increase Weight
You've crushed all sets on Bench Press 3 sessions in a row! Add 5 lbs next session — your muscles are ready for the challenge.
💡
Perfect Weight
You're at the perfect weight on Lat Pulldown! Hitting every set except the last is the sweet spot. Hold this weight until you can finish all sets.
📉
Drop the Weight
You've been missing sets on Romanian Deadlift. Try 10 lbs less — controlled reps with full range of motion build more muscle than struggling through.
🔄
Deload Week
5 weeks of hard training — time for a deload! Drop to 50% weight for 1 week. This isn't going backwards — it's how you come back stronger and hit new PRs.
